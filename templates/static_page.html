More Than a Label.

Redefining the modern man's wardrobe through style, substance, and sustainability.

Conscious Materials

Our commitment begins with the thread. We meticulously source premium, low-impact materials like GOTS-certified organic cotton, innovative recycled fibers, and breathable linen. We choose fabrics that feel incredible against your skin and are gentle on the environment, ensuring every garment starts with a foundation of integrity.

Ethical Craftsmanship

A great design is meaningless if it's not made with respect. We partner exclusively with certified factories that share our commitment to fair wages and safe working conditions. Our transparent supply chain ensures that every person who touches an AURA garment is treated with dignity, skill, and respect.

Timeless by Design

Fast fashion is a cycle of waste. We design to break it. Our collections are built around versatile, enduring pieces that move beyond fleeting trends. By focusing on superior fit and durable construction, we create clothing that is meant to be a staple in your wardrobe for years, not just a season.

{% elif content_key == "faq" %}

How do I find my correct size?

We recommend checking the size chart available on every product page. Our slim fit items run true to size, while our relaxed fit items offer a bit more room. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

How long does shipping take?

Standard domestic shipping typically takes 5-7 business days from the time your order is processed. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information as soon as your order has shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window for all unworn, unwashed, and defective items with tags still attached. You can learn more and start a return from our Return Policy page.

Are your products really sustainable?

Yes. Sustainability is at the core of everything we do at AURA. We prioritize the use of organic and recycled materials, partner with ethically certified factories, and are committed to minimizing our environmental impact. You can read more about our mission on our About Us page.
